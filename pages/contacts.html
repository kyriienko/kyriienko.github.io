The University of Exeter is a member of the Russell Group of research-intensive UK universities. It has over 23,000 students across four campuses in the picturesque South West part of Britain. QuDOS group resides in the Streatham Campus, north from the Exeter city centre. Our office is located in Physics Tower, which is considered one of the highest buildings in Exeter. The view from the top is truly breathtaking!
