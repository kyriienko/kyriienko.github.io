Open PhD position in Quantum Machine Learning

Link: https://www.jobs.ac.uk/job/CHJ772/

Application deadline: 30th July 2021

We have a PhD position opening to join the QuDOS group. We are searching for a candidate with strong mathematical background who will join the Quantum Machine Learning research line. The position will be funded as a part of Maths DTP sponsored by EPSRC, and will be assessed by the panel from the Department of Mathematics, University of Exeter.

The project description and application procedure is posted at jobs.ac.uk. To apply please follow to the University of Exeter postgraduate recruitment page.

Motivation: Despite the ongoing progress, the field of quantum machine learning (QML) is in the early development stage. While many heuristically-motivated approaches have shown promise, rigorous mathematical studies of QML remain rare. The reason behind is a complex mathematical description of processing the data embedded in a Hilbert space. An outstanding challenge is in understanding the mathematical structure of quantum circuits for data embedding. A progress in this direction will lead to groundbreaking changes in the way machine learning is performed on a quantum hardware.

In the project we will develop the theoretical background for constructing quantum circuits and efficient dataset loading. This will combine state-of-the-art techniques for analysing circuits and assessing the representation space of QML models. We will study the algorithmic complexity for data loading and model training, putting them on the rigorous ground. The results will aid high-quality quantum machine learning solutions, and in the future help solving relevant industrial problems.
